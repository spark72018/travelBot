NavBuddy

Get maps, directions, and traffic info delivered straight to your Slack channel

Add to Slack

Choose your favorite way to travel with our directions commands:

/drive

/walk

/bike

/transit

Just type your Start and Finish addresses separated by a ">":

/bike [Start] > [Finish]

Or, get the general layout of a location with the static map generator /mapme command:

/mapme [Address]

If a location is important to you, then let NavBuddy know with our /save command! The next time you want to use that address, just use the label you gave it:

/save [Name] > [Address]
