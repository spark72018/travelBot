MapMeBot

Get directions and map your locations straight from Slack

Add to Slack

Available commands:

/save [locationname] [address] (Save a location to reuse)

/mapme [address] (Get a static map image of entered address)

/mapmedrive [address] > [address] (Get driving directions)

/mapmewalk [address] > [address] (Get walking directions)

/mapmetransit [address] > [address] (Get transit directions)

/help (Get help!)
